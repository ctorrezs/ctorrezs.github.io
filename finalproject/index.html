Simple PC Builder

This mini-site lets you choose PC parts, see images, and calculate a total price.

This is the website my project was based on - PCPartPicker

Build Checklist

  1. Choose a CPU
  2. Select a motherboard
  3. Add memory (RAM)
  4. Pick your storage
  5. Select a graphics card (GPU)
  6. Choose a power supply (PSU)

Quick Tips

  • Make sure your power supply is strong enough.
  • Pick enough RAM for your games or programs.
  • Use an SSD for faster loading times.
  • Stay within your budget.
