My 5 Favorite Foods

Philly Cheesesteak

My first cheesesteak was from Charleys Cheesesteaks, and I thought it was really good. However, my little brother likes to cook, and when he made his own, it made Charleys Cheesesteaks look bad in comparison.

Lasagna Fritta

The Lasagna Fritta from Olive Garden is just labeled as an appetizer, but everytime we went I would always buy it as my main meal. The only complain I have is that they dont give you more per plate.

Lentils

My Mom makes the best Lentils hands down, and the best part is that she makes so much it feels endless. No image on the internet looks half as good as hers, so I just choose a raw lentils image.

Enchiladas Suizas

These are my favorite type of enchiladas. You can save the leftovers in the fridge, so they come in handy whenever I'm in a rush. Just heat them up in the microwave for a bit, and they're ready to go.
